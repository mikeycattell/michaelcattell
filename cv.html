Experience | Projects

I help multidisciplinary teams deliver services in a user centred way by designing accessible experiences.

I'm a design leader with 16 years of experience in the public and private sectors, currently working as Principal Interaction Design at Hippo Digital.

I have delivered projects to a range of Government organisations including the The Department for Work and Pensions, Universal Credits, NHS Digital and supporting the GDS design system.
At The Department for Work and Pensions I successfully led the design and delivery of a politically charged service go-live. Joining teams from multiple sites and disciplines to produce a challenging service.

I have also worked in various industries outside of Government organisations including Manchester City, Sky Bet, British Airways, Royal Mail, GOV.UK, Sky Sports, Argos, and Jet2.

In the past, I have worked contracting or as a permanent member working with col-located, distributed and fully remote teams. I pride myself on being very easy to work alongside and have often slotted smoothly into any team and delivered some great services and websites.

I have previously been SC cleared and I have BPSS clearance.
