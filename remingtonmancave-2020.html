Remington Mancave

The Problem

Mentioned on Yourstyle and Russell Hobbs case studies, I was connected to work on another piece for Remington. This one needed to aim almost exclusively for men, selling their "all in one shaver", the only restriction was to showcase the shaver off as best as possible.

The Solution

We went for the "mancave" approach, aiming to show how much money you could save using this shaver rather than going to a hairdressers, whilst trying to be witty at the same time. Visually, we used branding, great photography and a "mancave" vibe, one of my favourite projects I've completed.

My Responsibilites

Design, regular feedback sessions with client and account manager, design iterations, development (static html), presentating to stakeholders.
