Russell Hobbs

The Problem

I was approached by Spectrum Brands to assist in their re-branding work with Russell Hobbs and Remington Yourstyle and Mancave. Their need was to deliver a very strong brand website that would allow the user to upload photos of their items in their kitchen and win a prize.

The Solution

Leaning heavily on the brand, the design had to match the look and feel of a Russell Hobbs product and provide the user with a smooth way to view more products and ultimately lead to sales.
It was also one of Russell Hobbs first responsive projects, so it was not just a piece of design/build work, but also an educational piece.

My Responsibilities

Research, Prototype, Design, Site strategy, mockup iterations, custom graphics, development
