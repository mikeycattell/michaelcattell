Hey you

I’m a freelance user interface and interaction designer, lover of design and people.

I help multidisciplinary teams deliver services in a user centred way by designing accessible experiences.

As a interaction designer contractor, I mainly serve clients in the public sector, building my designs as working prototypes (in HTML/CSS/JavaScript) for usability testing.

I am currently contracted at Financial Authority Conduct instigating and implementing their first Design System until 1st April 2024.

I have worked in various industries on several high-profile projects, including NHS, Department of Work and Pensions (DWP), HMRC, Sky Bet, British Airways, Royal Mail, DEFRA, Sky Sports, Man City, and Jet2.
