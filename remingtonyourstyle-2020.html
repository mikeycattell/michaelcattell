Remington YourStyle

The Problem

After working on the Russell Hobbs website, I was also commissioned with Remington's YourStyle. A new product, which I was approached by Spectrum Brands to assist in their re-branding work with Russell Hobbs and Remington YourStyle and Mancave. Their need was to deliver a very strong brand website that would allow the user to upload photos of their items in their kitchen and win a prize.

The Solution

Having free-reign on the project, the design was leaning towards "what can this product do for you/it matches your style", giving the user the feeling of a personalised product that was made for them. As with Russell Hobbs, this project was one of their first responsive websites.

My Responsibilities

Research, Prototype, Design, Site strategy, mockup iterations, custom graphics, development
